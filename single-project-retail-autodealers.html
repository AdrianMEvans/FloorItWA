Barbagallo Automotive

This project was the renowned Barbagallo showroom, including Ferrari, Maserati, Aston Martin and Inifiniti. Aston Martin portion: engineered and stained oak by Havwood. A custom product used by Aston Martin globally. It is a beautifully stained oak and perfect for both residential or commercial uses. Infiniti portion utilised engineered Oak for the feature walls and a small portion of the lounge. Ferrari: oak engineered timber was installed in the display area. The sophisticated colour palette for the entire showroom reflects the brands prestige and luxurious image.
