FloorItWA was a dream which became a reality for Gavin and Roxy McGowan in 2014. Gavin always had a creative flair and combined with his absolute passion for carpentry the business catapulted over the last eight years. Today, even though the team has grown to ten full time members, the business is still owner managed and Gavin is personally involved with every job from start to completion.

Our team consists of skilled carpenters, trained concrete preparators and trade assistants. We have a Level 3 ATFA Accreditation and we proudly stand behind our products. We deliver superior customer service, dedicated support and superior customer service as well as product warranties encompassing the most comprehensive coverage in the industry. With decades of experience, we are confident in the craftsmanship and quality of our products.
