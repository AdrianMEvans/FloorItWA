Noranda Residence

This exciting transformation of a little girl's bedroom from mix match materials to luxury oak was handed to us as a blank canvas.The client had heard of our eye for design and asked us to come up with the whole bedroom design. Every piece in this design was created using Engineered European oak.

We couldn’t have been happier to accept the “innovative design award” at the ATFA awards night in Melbourne.
