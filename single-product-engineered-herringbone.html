Engineered Timber - Herringbone Range

Herringbone engineered timber floors are an excellent choice for those looking for a stylish and durable flooring option. The herringbone pattern is a classic design that adds a touch of sophistication and elegance to any room. Engineered timber floors are made with multiple layers of wood, making them more resistant to changes in temperature and humidity than solid wood floors. This makes them ideal for use in a variety of environments, including high-traffic areas and spaces with underfloor heating. In addition, herringbone engineered timber floors are easy to install and maintain, making them a practical and cost-effective choice for any home or commercial space.

When it comes to wooden floors, there are several types of layouts to choose from, each with its unique characteristics. The classic plank layout is the most common, featuring long, parallel boards that create a sense of spaciousness and simplicity. Another popular option is the chevron layout, which uses shorter boards arranged in a V-shape to create a more dynamic and visually interesting pattern.

One of the most elegant and timeless wooden floor layouts is the herringbone pattern, which features rectangular boards arranged in a zigzag pattern that creates a sense of movement and depth. This layout can be used to create a variety of effects, from traditional and rustic to modern and sophisticated, depending on the type of wood and finish used.
