Engineered Timber - Hamptons Range

Engineered timber has become a symbol of luxury and strength in the construction industry due to its unique properties. Unlike traditional timber, engineered timber is stronger, more stable, and resistant to shrinkage and warping. It's also a more sustainable option, as it uses less wood and can be produced with materials from sustainably managed forests. In addition to its structural benefits, engineered timber can be used to create visually stunning designs, adding warmth and natural beauty to any space. With its combination of strength and aesthetics, it's no wonder that engineered timber is such a popular choice for high-end construction projects.

Hamptons Range

The Hamptons Range of European Oak is a versatile and popular choice for homes and businesses in Perth. It comes in a wide range of different color schemes and features multi-layer plywood construction for lifetime stability.

Coated with European-made sealers and topcoats, it is both hard-wearing and easy to maintain. The range showcases genuine European Oak at an affordable price point, making it a high-quality and practical flooring option.
