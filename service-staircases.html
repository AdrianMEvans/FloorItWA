Staircases

Timber should be the material of choice for designers seeking internal staircases of strength, beauty and durability. Interior staircase work is considered a specialised area of carpentry and joinery as its construction requires high levels of workmanship, detail and accuracy.

Our flooring installation services cover you from project inception to completion of every detail.

To comply with building standards and insurance Floorit (WA) insists on “plying up/boxing” every concrete staircase prior to final cladding. This involves levelling and plumbing each tread and riser using structural plywood and industry standard adhesives. We can provide a wide range of profiles when it comes to nosing and stringers.
